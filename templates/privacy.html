Sign-in Demo Privacy Policy

This application demonstrates how to use Google Sign-in to authenticate visitors. If you choose to sign in with Google, the application will receive your email address.

User data is never stored on this server. Instead, your email address will be stored in your own web browser, in an encrypted cookie. That cookie will be sent to this application's server whenever a web page is requested. It will not be sent to other web sites.

The cookie will automatically be deleted when you close your browser.
